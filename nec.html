Admissions Requirements

  • Application Checklist

Audition

  • Prescreen Video Repertoire
    • A major movement of a concerto
    • The Prelude and any dance movement from a Bach Cello Suite
  • Audition Repertoire
    • A major movement of a concerto (memorized). For the Tchaikovsky Rococo, the full piece must be presented. For the Bloch Schelomo, rehearsal #20 through #30 must be included.
    • The Prelude and any dance movement from a Bach Cello Suite (memorized)
    • One of the following:
      • A showpiece such as Tchaikovsky Pezzo Capriccioso, Popper Elfentanz, or Davidoff At the Fountain
      • An Italian sonata by Boccherini, Valentini or Locatelli. One slow and one fast movement should be presented. No duo sonatas (Beethoven, Brahms, etc.) may be substituted in this category.
      • A Popper Etude or Piatti Caprice
